Movie Review

Banglore days

Rating :★★★★☆

Bangalore Days opens with a narrative style of Navin Pouli - the story is about 3 cousins and their different temperaments and lives. Though each personality is very different from one other, their bond which is portrayed through an excellent screenplay is what makes the film special
